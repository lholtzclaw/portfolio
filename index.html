Introduction

Hello, and welcome to my page!

I currently reside in Plano, Texas, where I am pursuing my interest in software development. Most recently, I worked as a store manager for the largest retail nursery company here in Texas. It was a blast and certainly challenging, but ultimately I found myself feeling unfulfilled in that role, and wishing to move towards a life that would bring me more joy. However, I have a huge passion for plants that will always be with me, no matter what I find myself doing! Learning how to code has been an interest of mine for the past couple of years that I just haven't been able to pursue because I was already so busy with the job I had. I'm so happy to have freed myself up and made the commitment to learn to be a software developer. Before working in the nursery industry, I worked in a variety of administrative roles and as a graduate assistant at the University of North Texas, as well as attended school there for both my undergraduate degree and for grad school. I want to become a web developer because there's so many aspects of it that draw me in. I love how many opportunities there are to be creative and solve problems. I like the challenge of using my brain in different ways to create something new that is useful. I'm excited to see where this journey takes me!

My favorite hobby of all has to be getting outdoors, out of my head, and deep into nature. I love camping, hiking, and exploring really any new place. I especially love areas that have a lot of natural vegetation. I also enjoy writing the occasional poem, drawing, and painting. Cooking up a tasty storm is another passion of mine. Given my love of plants, I also love to garden (patio style, too!) and have too many houseplants to count.

Bruce

Last, but not least, is the dear ole dog!
